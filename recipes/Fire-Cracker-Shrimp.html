Fire Cracker Shrimp

Description

  • You’ll find the full, step-by-step recipe below – but here’s a brief overview of what you can expect when you make firecracker shrimp at home:
  • Make the sauce.
  • Dry the shrimp, season, and coat with cornstarch.
  • Cook the shrimp in oil until opaque, then remove from the wok.
  • Boil the sauce until thick.
  • Return the shrimp to the wok and toss in the sauce.
  • Serve the firecracker shrimp over rice and garnish.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup sweet chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon lime zest
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha
  • 1 tablespoon sodium-reduced soy sauce
  • 1 pound medium shrimp (41 to 50), peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 teaspoons salt
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons peanut oil
  • 4 cups hot cooked white rice
  • 2 tablespoons bias-sliced green onions
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons toasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Stir together sweet chili sauce, lime zest, lime juice, sriracha, and soy sauce.
  3. Blot shrimp dry with paper towels and season with salt.
  4. Toss shrimp with cornstarch in a medium bowl to coat.
  5. Heat oil in a wok or large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add shrimp and cook until opaque, 2 to 3 minutes. Remove from wok.
  6. Add sauce mixture to the wok and heat until boiling.
  7. Return shrimp to the wok and toss to coat.
  8. Serve Firecracker Shrimp over rice. Garnish with green onion and sesame seeds.
