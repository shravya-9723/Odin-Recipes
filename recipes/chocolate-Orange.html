Chocolate-Orange Cupcakes with Pistachio Buttercream

Description

  • Prep Time:
  • 20 mins
  • Cook Time:
  • 20 mins
  • Additional Time:
  • 30 mins
  • Total Time:
  • 1 hr 10 mins
  • Servings:
  • 12
  • Yield:
  • 1 dozen cupcakes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour, sifted
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup cold water
  • ⅓ cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons orange juice
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon grated orange zest
  • Icing:
  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • ⅔ cup confectioners' sugar, sifted
  • 2 tablespoons instant pistachio pudding mix
  • 2 tablespoons cold water
  • 1 ounce dark chocolate, grated

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease 12 muffin cups with shortening and dust with flour, or line with paper liners.
  3. Combine 1 ½ cups flour, white sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in a mixing bowl.
  4. Beat in 1 cup cold water, olive oil, orange juice, and vanilla extract on medium-low speed until just combined. Fold in orange zest.
  5. Pour batter into prepared muffin cups, filling them ⅔ full.
  6. Bake for about 20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool completely on a wire rack.
  7. Beat butter with an electric mixer until fluffy. Gradually add confectioners’ sugar and beat until incorporated.
  8. Mix in pudding powder until just combined, then add water 1 tablespoon at a time until icing reaches the desired consistency.
  9. Ice the cooled cupcakes and garnish with grated chocolate.
